NPPE - Useful Resources

While reviewing the NPPE syllabus, I decided it would be a good idea to create a culminized list of resources for when needing to review content in the future, and for sharing with others wanting to prepare for the NPPE. I used the sections included in the syllabus as the format in which to organize the resources below. Please note that these resources may one day be outdated, and are written from the prespective of an individual in Ontario. There is no guarantee this page will be complete coverage of the content in the NPPE.
